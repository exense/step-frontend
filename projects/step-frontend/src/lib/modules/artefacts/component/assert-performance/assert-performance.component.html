Performance assertions

With this control you can make performance assertions.
The assertions are based on the measurements generated within and by your Keywords. Each Keyword execution generates at least one measurement named after the Keyword. In addition to the Keyword-Measurement so called custom Measurements measured within the Keyword are generated.
With this control you can for instance assert that the average response time of a particular Keyword is less than a specific threshold.
